planetarium
The assignment was to design a Planetarium but with the freedom to define any more program. The site analysis and study of the possible circulation, limit roads, surrounding distinct buildings, sighting to the landscape (to the Dom and the fjords), the potential program; were represented by lines and schemes that gradually generated movements. This movements generate a form, a fluid single element that adapts to the different site characteristics. The idea was to design an attractive building by the form with a vast program, containing different rooms like, for example, a gravity zero room, an auditorium, or science interactive museum.
