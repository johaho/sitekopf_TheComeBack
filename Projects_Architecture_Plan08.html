plan 08
For the Plan08, Architecture Week in Cologne, our office participated with a light installation in the iconic telecommunication tower and simultaneously with an exposition in the entrance pavilion, regarding the tower history and panorama. All office co-workers were challenged to design his own light installation idea, so to exhibit. One of the ideas that I decided to develop was more than a light installation. It was a light colour dance performed on the air by the Catalan group Fura del Baus. This performative group is able to create interesting places and ambiences by just playing with the light and moving through all the dimensional space.
