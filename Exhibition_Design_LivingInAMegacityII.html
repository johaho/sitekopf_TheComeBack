living in a megacity II

The exhibition was the assemble of the concept "Living in a Megacity". It integrated a conference cycle about Megacities organised by Institute of Geography from the Cologne University, and which toke place in the Wissenschaftszentrum in Bonn, Germany, in April 2013. The aim was to present the researched work done by the Institute of Geography in the cities Guangzhou and Dhaka, and parallel show them with Tokyo, the great world wide megacity.
Hence the target audience of the exhibition was mainly experts, it was fundamental the collaboration of the Institute of Geography, as well as from other researchers, in order to obtain accurate and "in situ" information about the studied cities.
Hereby it was possible to test same of the "Living in a Megacity" main ideas.
